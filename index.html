About me



Hello, my name is Alamsyah. I was born in Makassar. I want to share a story about when I was still in my mother’s womb. According to my parents, I am the fifth child. During the pregnancy, my mother became paralyzed because her belly was too large. Some said it was due to twin water pregnancy, and it turned out to be true. My siblings and father helped my mother eat, drink, and use the bathroom. When it was time for my mother to give birth, my parents didn’t have the money for the delivery. My father asked relatives for help, but no one could offer any. He then prayed to Almighty God, Allah SWT, to grant us fortune — and miraculously, he received some money, which he immediately used to take my mother for treatment and delivery. As a child, I lived a simple life. Sometimes there were arguments between my parents, even on my birthday. When I entered elementary school, I found myself in a bad environment where I was bullied. This continued into junior high school. Eventually, I started isolating myself during vocational high school and beyond. People often misread my face as unmotivated or expressionless, but they don’t know what I’ve truly felt inside. I think I’ll stop here — the story is already quite long.
